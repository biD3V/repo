Pinnie

Pin messages like iOS 14! Swipe left on a conversation to pin and hard press on a pin to get an unpin option.
Note: On iOS 11/12 long hold on the pin and release to unpin.
For quicker mass removing tap on the three dots in the top right, "Select Messages," then tap the red minus on the pins you want to remove.

Customization Options

  • Normal or Compact Layout
  • Set the size of Normal layout between small and large
  • Change number of columns on Normal layout
  • Disable the "Drop Glow" and change it's opacity
